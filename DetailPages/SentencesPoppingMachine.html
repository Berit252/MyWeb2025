Sentences Popping Machine (Exhibition View)

Throughout history, controversies and topics have never been scarce. They serve as essential indicators of the cultural aspects of human civilization in specific regions and eras. However, in today’s age of globalization, cultural transformations are rapidly accelerating. As a result, viewpoints and beliefs can easily face critique and disintegration.

在历史的长河中，争议与话题从未缺席，它们是反映人类在特定地区及年代文化的关键标志。然而，在当今全球化的大背景下，人文变革的速度愈发加快，言论导向与信念在极短的时间内便可能遭到质疑和崩坏。


In this era, navigating through the vast amount of information feels like wandering in a maze without signs. This topics/messages, are designed to capture our attention, personalized to cater to our preferences, aims to bring us joy. Yet, it also brings annoyance, just like how everyone suggests apples are healthy, but I find myself averse to their taste.

在这个时代，我们每日接收的信息量巨大，如同在一个无标识的迷宫中漫步。这些信息旨在吸引我们的注意力，针对我们的喜好进行个性化定制，旨在为我们带来快乐。然而，这同样也带来了困扰，正如即便众人皆说苹果有益健康，我却因不喜欢其味道而拒之门外


This work (a video based on a website I built) seeks to explore how information becomes increasingly complex and layered, ultimately leading to its own deconstruction. This reflects the current state of our world, where we are bombarded with information and constantly trying to maintain a balance. Meanwhile, how we process information also reflects our understanding of and response to the world around us. Through this project, I gained insight into the ways we consume and process information, and how it relates to our personal beliefs and cultural orientations.

该作品意在探讨当信息被过量堆叠时，它们最终会如何自行解构，反映出我们所处的时代特征——一个信息爆炸且迅速变化的世界。在这个世界里，我们不断试图保持平衡。于此同时，我们对信息的处理方式，也反映了我们对周遭世界的理解和反应。通过这个项目，我对当前信息消费方式的进行了反思，对个人信念和社会文化导向有了另一种思考。

MATERIAL: paper, cardboard, electric wires, iPhone screen, keyboard, tin foil
